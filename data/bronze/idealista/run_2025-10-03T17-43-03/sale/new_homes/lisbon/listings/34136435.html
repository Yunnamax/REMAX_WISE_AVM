Selva is the new private condominium being built in the vibrant riverside neighborhood of Marvila, currently one of the most dynamic and fastest-growing areas in Lisbon.

Designed by Appleton & Domingos studio, Selva presents an innovative approach to urban living, where nature, culture, and community spirit intertwine in an industrial-inspired environment overlooking the Tagus River.

Selva comprises four buildings, a 12,000m² private condominium consisting of apartments ranging from T1 to T5, with areas between 50m² and 250m².

The interiors stand out for their high ceilings, which guarantee a feeling of spaciousness, and private outdoor spaces, such as gardens, balconies, and terraces. Each apartment has parking with pre-installation for electric vehicle charging and most have storage.

Selva's amenities further enhance the experience of living in this condominium: a swimming pool, Turkish bath, coworking space, multi-purpose condominium room, and a fitness area were designed to meet the demands of contemporary life, promoting well-being and daily balance.

The landscaping project, by Baldios studio, transforms the industrial past of this location into an urban oasis, with common gardens, rest areas, and green spaces that invite relaxation, socializing, and contact with nature.

Marvila is today one of Lisbon's most creative hubs. The neighborhood attracts artists, entrepreneurs, and families seeking an authentic experience, with character and accessibility.

Among contemporary art galleries, craft breweries, cafes, and cultural spaces like the Fábrica Braço de Prata, Marvila preserves its original identity, where traditional businesses coexist with new projects in a genuinely Lisbon atmosphere.

Excellent accessibility and direct connection to the Braço de Prata train station in one of the city's most promising areas.

Castelhana is now Dils.

Castelhana is a leading name in the Portuguese real estate sector for over 25 years and is now Dils, an Italian company with over 50 years of history and offices in Milan, Rome, and Amsterdam, and now in Lisbon, Porto, Algarve, and Estoril.

In Lisbon, our headquarters are located in Chiado, one of the city's most emblematic and traditional neighborhoods. In Porto, we are located on the iconic Avenida da Boavista. In the Algarve region, we have an office near the Vilamoura Marina, and in Estoril, next to the famous Estoril Casino.

Under the Dils brand, we specialize in consulting for companies, organizations, and institutional investors, offering services for the purchase, sale, rental, and development of residential properties.

We are at your disposal, with a dedicated team to offer the best support in your next real estate investment.
